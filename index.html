About SupaQ Hotel & Event Centre

Welcome to SupaQ Hotel & Event Centre, your destination for comfort, relaxation and memorable experiences. Located at 6/8 Supaq Street, Off Palace Road, Ilogbo-Eremi, via Oko-Afo Bus Stop, Badagry Express Road, Lagos State, we provide luxury accommodation, delicious meals, premium drinks, event hosting, and entertainment in a peaceful environment.



🛏 Luxury Accommodation

Enjoy comfortable, clean and fully furnished rooms designed to give you a relaxing stay.

🎉 Event Centre

Host weddings, birthdays, seminars, meetings, receptions and corporate events in our beautiful event hall.

🍽 Restaurant & Bar

Taste freshly prepared Nigerian and continental dishes with premium drinks served every day.

🎱 Snooker Lounge

Relax with friends and enjoy our professional snooker tables in a comfortable atmosphere.
